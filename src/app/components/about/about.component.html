About us

This is a web application that resembles a To Do application like Microsoft To Do. For the front-end I used angular and different angular components and for the back-end, I used .NET 6. Bellow I will do a little guide through out the application also responding to popular questions.
Enjoy the application and thank you for taking your time to browse my wep application.

This application is a space where users who have an account can make their to do lists, groceries lists, set up various goals and many more...
They can individualy set for each to do item a due date, a yellow star if the to do item it's important, or to check/uncheck the item when that particular item it's done.

To register to this application you need a valid email address (you can only have one account per email address), choose a display name and a strong password (example of strong password: Pa$$w0rd).

Let's talk a little bit about the basic functionalities of the application:

  • Click on your name (top right corner) to log out of the application.
  • Click the plus icon to add a new to do card
  • Click the trash icon to delete either the to do card or a single to do item
  • Click the star in order to make a to do item important (if the star is yellow, the item is marked as important).
  • Click the square icon to mark a to do as done (if the square has a check inside it, the item is marked as done).

In order to edit a to do card you have to click on the title you want to edit, at the end press this icon to save your edits If you want to edit a to do item, just click on it and start to edit, when you click out of the edit box it will save automatically.
